Mission Statement


At BC course map, we understand the student's struggle and confusion with planning their academic journey. With the vast variety of classes offered along with the minute details that students have to get a grasp of, finding the right classes can be frustrating. Through using our website, you will have access to all the class information you need gathered in one place and have a better understanding of course-planning.


We dedicate this platform to Brooklyn College students who struggle with course-planning. Whether you're a freshman or returnee, we hope to guide you to the right path of graduation with the exception of course-planning frustrations. Start exploring our webpage today!
